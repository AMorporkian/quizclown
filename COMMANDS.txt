Quizclown commands

!hint
 Give a hint for the current question.
 Throttled.

!ask or !next
 Short-circuit question asking delay.

!skip or !next
 Vote to skip the current question.

!squit
 (owner only) save game and shut down quizclown.

!quit
 (owner only) shut down quizclown.

!clear
 (owner only) clear scores.

!score or !scores
 Show current scores. 
 Gets invoked automatically every 100 seconds;
 throttled.
 
!reshuffle
 Re-shuffle the questions and restart
 asking them.

!info
 Show commands, source code URL, questions source.
