Quizclown commands

!hint
 Give a hint for the current question.
 Throttled.

!ask
 Short-circuit question asking delay.

!skip or !next
 Vote to skip the current question.

!squit
 (owner only) save game and shut down quizclown.

!quit
 (owner only) shut down quizclown.

!score or !scores
 Show current scores. 
 Gets invoked automatically every 100 seconds;
 throttled.
